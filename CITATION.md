# Citations and Attributions

This project builds upon the shoulders of giants in the machine learning, industrial IoT, and open-source communities. We use public datasets to simulate our factory scenarios. Please cite and attribute the original authors if you use this codebase for research.

## Datasets Used

### 1. MIMII Dataset (Sound Anomalies)
The **MIMII** (Malfunctioning Industrial Machine Investigation and Inspection) dataset is used for acoustic anomaly detection.
* **Paper**: Harsh Purohit, Ryo Tanabe, Kenji Ichige, Takashi Endo, Yuki Nikaido, Kaori Suefusa, and Yohei Kawaguchi, “MIMII Dataset: Sound Dataset for Malfunctioning Industrial Machine Investigation and Inspection,” in Proc. 4th Workshop on Detection and Classification of Acoustic Scenes and Events (DCASE), 2019.
* **Link**: [MIMII Dataset](https://zenodo.org/record/3384388)
* **License**: CC-BY-4.0

### 2. MVTec AD Dataset (Visual Anomalies)
The **MVTec AD** dataset is used for visual defect detection on factory parts.
* **Paper**: Paul Bergmann, Michael Batzner, Michael Hellstern, and Carsten Steger, "MVTec AD – A Comprehensive Real-World Dataset for Unsupervised Anomaly Detection", IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR), 2019.
* **Link**: [MVTec AD](https://www.mvtec.com/company/research/datasets/mvtec-ad/)
* **License**: CC-BY-NC-SA-4.0

### 3. NASA CMAPSS Dataset (Turbofan Degradation)
The **CMAPSS** dataset is used for simulating sensor drift and remaining useful life (RUL) estimation.
* **Paper**: A. Saxena, K. Goebel, D. Simon, and N. Eklund, "Damage Propagation Modeling for Aircraft Engine Run-to-Failure Simulation", in the Proceedings of the 1st International Conference on Prognostics and Health Management (PHM08), Denver CO, Oct 2008.
* **Link**: [NASA Ames Prognostics Data Repository](https://ti.arc.nasa.gov/tech/dash/groups/pcoe/prognostic-data-repository/)
